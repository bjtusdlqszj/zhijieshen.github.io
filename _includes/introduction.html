Kang Liao 廖康

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU) in MePro, advised by Professor Chunyu Lin. I have been fortunate to work with Yunchao Wei at BJTU, Xiangyu Xu at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Yulan Guo at National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), and Moncef Gabbouj at Tampere University. Recently I am conducting my research at Max Planck Institute for Informatics in Computer Graphics group advised by Vahid Babaei (Oct. 2021 - Oct. 2022).

I was borned and raised in Anqing, a picturesque city with colorful culture in China.

Research Interests

My research interest lies at the intersection of Computer Vision and Deep Learning. In particular, my work focuses on image generation/restoration, multiple view geometry, 3D vision and point cloud, and 360° panoramic scene understanding, etc.
